Why Us
100% Accuracy

Our platform works with Multiple API's and takes the average of them to provide the most accurate weather conditions. Besides that we are working with experts under the hood which allows us to display the most accurate weather.

Extreme Weathers

Facing extreme weathers unexpectedly is a sad story. It might sometimes result in bad consequenses. When the user searches for their city if the weather is too high or too low they will be informed to take certain actions so that they wont end in problems.

Extended Time-Period

Sometimes the user wants to know the multiple weathers. We allow our users to check the other cities weather conditons without needing them to type the city name because its possible to see the city they are searching for in the moving bar.

How It Works
